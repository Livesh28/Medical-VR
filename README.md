# 🩺 VR Venipuncture Training Simulator

A Unity-based Virtual Reality (VR) training application that simulates the complete venipuncture (blood collection) procedure in an immersive and interactive environment. The simulator provides step-by-step guidance, instructional videos, annotations, and real-time interaction, allowing learners to safely practice the procedure without risk to patients.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Venipuncture is one of the most commonly performed medical procedures. Traditional training methods often require supervision, consumables, and repeated practice on patients or mannequins.

This project provides an immersive VR learning experience where users can perform the entire venipuncture procedure in a virtual environment while receiving visual guidance and real-time feedback.

The simulator follows standard clinical workflow and is designed for educational purposes.

- Provide safe and repeatable venipuncture training.

# 🏥 Procedure Workflow

The simulator follows the standard venipuncture procedure.

## Step 1
### Hand Wash
- Clean hands before starting.

---

## Step 2
### Wear Gloves
- Wear sterile gloves.

---

## Step 3
### Apply Tourniquet
- Apply the tourniquet above the puncture site.

---

## Step 4
### Secure Tourniquet
- Position the tourniquet correctly.

---

## Step 5
### Clean Injection Site
- Clean the skin using cotton.

---

## Step 6
### Apply Spirit
- Disinfect the puncture area.

---

## Step 7
### Locate Vein
- Identify the suitable vein.

---

## Step 8
### Insert Cannula
- Carefully insert the cannula.

---

## Step 9
### Secure Cannula
- Place the cannula correctly.

---

## Step 10
### Attach Collection Tube
- Connect the blood collection tube.

---

## Step 11
### Secure Tube
- Ensure proper placement.

---

## Step 12
### Remove Tourniquet
- Release the tourniquet.

---

## Step 13
### Remove Collection Tube
- Detach the collection tube.

---

## Step 14
### Remove Cannula
- Carefully remove the cannula.

---

## Step 15
### Place Tube in Stand
- Store the collected sample safely.

---

## Step 16
### Dispose Biohazard Waste
- Dispose materials in Yellow Bin.

---

## Step 17
### Dispose Sharps Waste
- Dispose needles in Red Bin.
